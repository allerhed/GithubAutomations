Chat Widget Demo

Interactive lead capture through conversational interface

Demo Mode: Click the chat button in the bottom-right corner to start a conversation. The widget will guide you through capturing lead information.

How it works:

  • Click the chat icon to open the widget
  • Answer the bot's questions to provide your information
  • The lead is automatically captured and assigned based on rules
  • In production, this would integrate with your CRM pipeline

Features:

  • Conversational UI for better engagement
  • Progressive information gathering
  • Email validation
  • Optional fields support
  • Customizable styling and position
